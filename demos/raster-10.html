{% set html_title="Hidden surface removal demo - Computer Graphics from Scratch" %} # Hidden surface removal demo This demo implements [Depth Buffering](../12-hidden-surface-removal.html#depth-buffering) and [Back Face Culling](../12-hidden-surface-removal.html#back-face-culling). You can turn them on and off to see the effect they have on the output. With Depth Buffering and Back Face Culling disabled, the output depends on the order of the triangles within the model. To show this, you can shuffle the triangles; and to make the output clearer, you can choose to draw the outlines of the individual triangles.
